Case Study

Shopify Podcast

Podcast Boost Through Targeted Email Outreach

Client Overview

Our client is the host of a podcast focused on helping eCommerce vendors discover innovative Shopify apps. The podcast invites Shopify app owners to share insights about their products, fostering a valuable knowledge-sharing platform for the eCommerce community.

Challenges

  • Finding relevant Shopify app owners interested in appearing on the podcast.
  • Creating a consistent flow of qualified guests to maintain the podcast's content schedule.
  • Generating interest without spamming potential guests or damaging the podcast’s reputation.

Our Strategy

To address these challenges, we implemented a multi-domain, multi-mailbox cold email campaign strategy:

  • Targeted Outreach: We curated a list of Shopify app owners who align with the podcast’s theme and audience interests.
  • Multi-Domain Approach:
    • Leveraged 1 domain and 5 mailboxes to manage the email load and maintain deliverability.
    • Ensured each mailbox sent only 20 emails/day, avoiding spam filters.
  • Compelling Email Content:
    • Designed personalized emails emphasizing the mutual benefits of joining the podcast.
    • A/B tested subject lines and CTAs to maximize engagement.
  • Consistent Follow-ups: Sent polite follow-ups to unresponsive contacts, keeping the outreach non-intrusive.

Results

  • Open Rate: Consistently achieved a 50% open rate (well above industry standards).
  • Positive Replies: Maintained a 3-5% positive reply rate, yielding 3–5 qualified guests weekly.
  • Seasonal Surge: Observed a significant increase in opens and replies starting in August, indicating strategic timing and improved targeting.
  • Client Success: The client’s podcast guest pipeline is thriving, leading to higher-quality episodes and increased audience engagement.

Key Takeaways

  • Focused Targeting Works: Reaching out to the right audience with tailored messaging is crucial for response rates.
  • Email Warm-Up Is Vital: Distributing emails across domains and mailboxes ensures high deliverability.
  • Data-Driven Optimization: Regularly refining the approach based on engagement metrics can lead to consistent improvements.
